Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is considered to be the greatest architectural achievement in the whole range of Indo-Islamic architecture. Its recognised architectonic beauty has a rhythmic combination of solids and voids, concave and convex and light shadow; such as arches and domes further increases the aesthetic aspect. Not a piece of architecture, as other buildings are, but the proud passions of an emperor’s love wrought in living stones.

Varanasi Temple

Kashi Vishwanath Temple is one of the most famous temples in India dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is located in the holy city of Varanasi on the banks of the Ganga River.The temple is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas, which are very sacred to Hindus.It is believed that visiting this temple brings spiritual liberation (moksha).The temple has a golden spire that shines beautifully in sunlight.Millions of devotees visit it every year from all over the world. The surrounding area is always filled with chants, bells, and devotion.
